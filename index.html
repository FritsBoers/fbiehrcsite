About Me

Hi there! I'm a passionate Hard Rock Cafe fan from the Netherlands, and my journey started in the mid-90s. What began as a casual visit to my first Hard Rock Café in Los Angeles soon grew into a lifelong passion traveling the world to collect pins and classic t-shirts from every Hard Rock Cafe, Hotel, and Rock Shop I come across.

With over 100 visits, more than 140 pins, and over 100 t-shirts in my collection, each item tells its own story. Through this site, I want to share that passion with fellow collectors and Hard Rock fans around the world.

Whether you're a seasoned collector or just curious, I hope this inspires you to visit a Hard Rock Cafe on your next trip.
