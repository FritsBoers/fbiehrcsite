🎸 HRC Collection — Location Data Converter

Fill in your locations in Excel using the template CSV, then paste the CSV content below to convert it to locations.json.

How to use:
1. Open data/locations-template.csv in Excel
2. Add all your locations (one per row)
3. Save as CSV (File → Save As → CSV UTF-8)
4. Open the CSV in Notepad, select all, copy
5. Paste into the box below and click Convert
